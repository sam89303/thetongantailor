FAQ

Questions worth answering upfront.

How long does a fitting take?

Roughly 30–40 minutes for measurements and fabric selection. No rush, no pressure — we'll take the time to get it right.

What's the turnaround?

7 days for Prince Range (made-to-measure), 21 days for King Range (fully custom) — from your fitting to a finished suit, ready for collection or delivery.

Do I need to be in Sydney?

Fittings are based in Sydney and regional NSW by appointment. If you're further out, get in touch — we can work out a fitting day that suits.

What's included in the price?

"From $895" covers a base Prince Range suit. Final price depends on the range (Prince vs King), fabric choice, occasion (wedding/event tailoring can involve premium cloth), and any rush turnaround. We'll walk you through this before anything's confirmed.

Do you take club or bulk orders?

Yes — for clubs, teams, or corporate groups, we run bulk fittings on-site. Get in touch and we'll organize a time that works for the whole group.

Is there a deposit?

Yes, a 50% deposit is required to begin your order, with the balance due on collection.
